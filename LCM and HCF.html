🔢 LCM & HCF Calculator 🔢

HCF (Highest Common Factor) is the biggest number that divides all the given numbers exactly.

LCM (Lowest Common Multiple) is the smallest number that is a multiple of all the given numbers.

Example:
Take 4 and 6.
Multiples of 4 are 4, 8, 12, 16...
Multiples of 6 are 6, 12, 18...
The smallest common multiple is 12. So LCM is 12.
The highest number that divides both 4 and 6 is 2. So HCF is 2.
