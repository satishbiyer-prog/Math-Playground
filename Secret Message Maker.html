🔐 Secret Message Maker 🔐

A Substitution Cipher is a secret way of writing messages where each letter is replaced with another letter.

For example, if you choose a shift of 3, A becomes D, B becomes E and so on.

So the word CAT would be written as FDW. Choose your secret shift and turn English into code!
